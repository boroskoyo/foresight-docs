With Foresight, you can easily list your and analyze your CI workflows in a repository. Foresight provides a high-level 
overview of performance across all your workflow runs.

Monitoring workflow success rates and run durations, you can identify where to focus for troubleshooting and 
debugging for specific workflow runs.
